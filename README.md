# ETI Educom - Institute Management System

A comprehensive institute management system with role-based dashboards, lead tracking, enrollment management, payments, and AI-powered business insights.

## Features
- Multi-role dashboards (Super Admin, Branch Admin, FDE, Counsellor, Trainer)
- Lead management & follow-up tracking
- Student enrollment & batch management
- Payment tracking & fee collection
- AI-powered business insights (GPT-4o)
- Quiz & exam management
- Reports & analytics
